## Overview

Development of a 2D shooter game using Unity, showcasing engaging gameplay mechanics, smooth animations, and polished visuals through the integration of sprite sheets.

## Key Features

- **Smooth Animations with Sprite Sheets**: Utilized a custom sprite sheet to create fluid character animations, including movement, shooting, and enemy interactions.  
- **Engaging Gameplay Mechanics**: Designed core mechanics such as shooting, enemy spawning, health management, and score tracking to deliver an action-packed experience.  
- **Level Design**: Created dynamic levels with varying difficulty, incorporating obstacles, power-ups, and enemy waves to enhance gameplay depth.  
- **Sound Effects and Visual Feedback**: Integrated sound effects and visual cues for actions like shooting, collisions, and power-ups to provide a more immersive experience.  

## Technologies Used

- **Unity Engine**: For developing the game, handling physics, and implementing gameplay logic.  
- **C#**: To script game mechanics, player controls, and enemy AI behavior.  
- **Sprite Sheets**: For character animations, environmental assets, and special effects.  
- **GarageBand**: To create and edit custom sound effects for the game.  

## Outcome

The 2D shooter game delivers an engaging, visually appealing, and action-packed experience. It highlights proficiency in Unity, C#, and animation techniques while demonstrating a strong understanding of game design principles.
